Welcome to Candle Realm

Create your own, unique candle experience...

We are not just making candles, but also bringing warmth into your home!

Our candles are all made by hand and all of our products have been tested.

We incourage people to go with healthy alternatives such as Beewswax or Soy Wax but we also stock Paraffin wax.

Have a look below at some of the key differences between those three:

BeesWax

Oldest material used for candles. Beeswax candles have even been found in the pyramids of ancient Egypt!

Beeswax is an all-natural, non-toxic substance used to make bee honeycombs.

Not used often for scented candles as it has its own honey aroma.

Soy Wax

Comes from soybean oil which is more sustainable and environmentally friendly.

It is a great choice for those wanting to live the vegan/vegetarian lifestyle.

Soy wax is often blended with other waxes to capitalize on the best of both waxes.

Known to have mild softer fragrance.

Paraffin Wax

The most common material for the mass market simply for its price.

It comes with a lot of downsides to your health and environment.

It is a final byproduct of petroleum.

Known to produce the strongest fragrances.
