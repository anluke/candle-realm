Some of the key differences between Beeswax, Soy Wax and Paraffin Wax

BeesWax

Oldest material used for candles. Beeswax candles have even been found in the pyramids of ancient Egypt! Beeswax is an all-natural, non-toxic substance used to make bee honeycombs. Not used often for scented candles as it has its own honey aroma.

Soy Wax

Comes from soybean oil which is more sustainable and environmentally friendly. It is a great choice for those wanting to live the vegan/vegetarian lifestyle. Soy wax is often blended with other waxes to capitalize on the best of both waxes. Known to have mild softer fragrance.

Paraffin Wax

The most common material for the mass market simply for its price. It comes with a lot of downsides to your health and environment. It is a final byproduct of petroleum. Known to produce the strongest fragrances.
