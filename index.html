Have a look below at some of the main Wax components that we use and their benefits:

BeesWax

Oldest material used for candles. Beeswax candles have even been found in the pyramids of ancient Egypt!

Beeswax is an all-natural, non-toxic substance used to make bee honeycombs.

Not used often for scented candles as it has its own honey aroma.

Soy Wax

Comes from soybean oil which is more sustainable and environmentally friendly.

It is a great choice for those wanting to live a vegan/vegetarian lifestyle.

Soy wax is often blended with other waxes to capitalize on the best of both waxes.

Known to have a mild softer fragrance.

Paraffin Wax

The most common material for the mass market simply for its price.

Not the healthiest option but still very easy to work with and accessible.

It is a final byproduct of petroleum.

Known to produce the strongest fragrances.
