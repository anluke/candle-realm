Who we are?

We are professionals in creating candles out of various compounds.

With many years of experience and testing, we have found the best and easiest way to build candles.

We have gathered a large following of customers who are interested in candle making and also their general wellbeing.

Why Us?

Candles found in supermarkets are generally made from cheap ingredients, mass-produced, and are hazardous for your health.

We aim to make the candles not just better but also healthier for you and your family.

Candles made out of BeesWax and Soy are natural and aligned with nature. They last longer and provide a better experience.

Our History

Candle Realm was founded in 2003 in Dublin, Ireland.

We started in a small office but now moved to larger premises in Sandyford, Dublin 18.

Since our opening, we have had a huge interest in candle-making kits and a lot of testimonials from our customers telling us how easy and practical it is.

Our staff members have years of knowledge and experience and are always trying out new things so we can extend our range and serve our customers better.
